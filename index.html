About

This composter was designed with an average urban house in mind. It allows the user to properly and efficiently decompose all compostable waste produced within the household. Through our smartphone app, you have the power to control your personal Smart Composter in the palm of your hands. Simply start/stop the composting process, gain insight on various statistics for your composter, or even optimize the composting to suit your household through your mobile device.

Automation

The Smart Composter provides automatic heating and mixing so there's very litle work done by you.

The Composter utilizes various sensors and peripherals to adjust its own settings in order to achieve time and erengy efficieny throughout the composting process.

Modern Design

With a nicely comapct size of A x B x C, the conveniently designed Food Cycler can easily be moved around the house and emptied when necessary.

Mobile Compatibility

The Smart Composter can be connected to your iOS devices via Bluetooth to give you the power to control in the palm of your hands.

The Bluetooth connecitivity will give you control within a radius of X metres from the Food Cycler.

Analytics

Analyze the composting process as it happens, gain insight on your composting history, and use past statistics to help you compost more efficiently in the future.
