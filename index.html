About

This composter was designed with an average urban house in mind. It allows the user to properly and efficiently decompose all compostable waste produced within the household.

Through our smartphone app, you have the power to control your personal Smart Composter in the palm of your hands. Simply start/stop the composting process, gain insight on various statistics for your composter, or even optimize the composting to suit your household through your mobile device.
